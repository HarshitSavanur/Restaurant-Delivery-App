Our Capstone Project

We are excited to present our capstone project, a food delivery system! Our motivation for this project came from the growing trend of online food ordering and delivery, especially during the COVID-19 pandemic. We aim to develop a user-friendly platform that allows customers to easily order their favorite meals from local restaurants and have them delivered to their doorstep. With this project, we hope to contribute to the convenience and safety of food delivery, and help support local businesses in the process.
